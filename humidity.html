Humidity is in simple terms the amount of water vapor in the air When we talk of the humidity from a weather perspective , we are talking of relative humidity Relative humidity is the amount of moisture in the air compared to what the air can "hold" at that temperature. When the air can't "hold" all the moisture, then it condenses as dew.
